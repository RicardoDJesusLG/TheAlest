D I S C O V E R   C U L T U R E

Must-See Museums in Mexico City.

National Museum of Anthropology

An essential journey through Mexico’s pre-Hispanic history. Home to the Sun Stone and vast collections.

Casa Azul (Frida Kahlo Museum)

The iconic Coyoacán residence that tells the story of the legendary Frida Kahlo’s life and work.

Palacio de Bellas Artes

A cultural symbol of Mexico. Admire murals by Rivera and Orozco and its spectacular architecture.

Templo Mayor

Discover the ceremonial center of ancient Mexico-Tenochtitlan—an archaeological journey in the city’s heart.

Soumaya Museum

An impressive contemporary building housing the vast art collection of the Carlos Slim Foundation.

Chapultepec Castle

Home to the National Museum of History. Walk through royal halls and enjoy panoramic views.

Jumex Museum

A space dedicated to contemporary art, with cutting-edge exhibitions and a world-class collection.

Rufino Tamayo Museum

Focused on international modern and contemporary art in addition to works by Oaxacan artist Rufino Tamayo.

Casa Estudio Luis Barragán

A masterpiece of modern architecture. A UNESCO World Heritage Site and a must for designers.

Other museums you can visit: Museum of Modern Art, Muralism Museum, Frida Kahlo Red House.
