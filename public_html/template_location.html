Polanco

The Alest Hotel is located in one of the most exclusive neighborhoods in Mexico City

Polanco is home to a multitude of high-end boutiques, the most sought-after urban residences, the most important museums in the capital city, and a vibrant and cosmopolitan atmosphere.

For those in search of a personalized shopping experience, the hotel's Concierge Service can coordinate sessions by appointment or behind closed doors in one of the most prestigious department stores or even in the high-end boutiques of your choice, and you may even purchase accessories or other items to be delivered at the hotel.

Its convenient location is ideal for planning visits to all the points of interest that the city offers.

Given the array of activities, attractions, and must-see landmarks that converge in the city, designing the ideal itinerary becomes easy with guidance from the experienced Concierge Service staff at The Alest.

Under The Alest Signature Experiences program, guests have access to exclusive tours with a bilingual guide and private transportation, and they may choose between walks and tours within Mexico City or visits to other cities and archaeological sites.

Experience Polanco

Polanco is a central hub that allows for indulging in different types of experiences to please all types of travelers. In this neighborhood, or very close to its streets, the best things happen. The most renowned events, such as the Haute Horlogerie Salon (SIAR), Zona MACO, Mercedes-Benz Fashion Week, the Longines Global Champions Tour equestrian meet, Design Week, the FYJA Polanco en Flores floral arrangement event, the alebrijes parade, the Gay Parade, and other events take place a few meters or a short distance from the hotel.
