rooms & suits

A universe onto itself, a haven for disconnecting

The Alest it offers an intimate, cozy space featuring 19 lavishly decorated suites. Three are Junior Suites, with large terraces that are perfect for enjoying the outdoors.

Enjoy sophisticated residential comfort in one of the 19 rooms. Each room offers comfort and refinement, reflected in its timeless design and neutral colors that induce relaxation. 10 rooms face an indoor patio and 9 offer outdoor views.

Their surface area ranges from 22 m2 (72 sq ft) to 75.59 m2 (247 sq ft) to accommodate from 2 to 4 people, according to chosen category.

The three exclusive Junior Suites located on the third floor have their own large terrace on the upper parts of the building, overlooking the pleasant and quiet Eugenio Sue Street, in the heart of Polanco. Their surface area ranges from 51.89m2 (170 sq ft) to 75.59 m2 (247 sq ft).

Rooms boast unique pieces of art and locally designed objects. The one-of-a-kind dressers in each room. Carrara marble bathrooms, Italian-style hardwood floors, and amenities that will make a stay at The Alest a memorable experience.
