Polanco, the hotel’s exclusive neighborhood
Location

Prime spot in Mexico City

Located in one of the most exclusive neighborhoods in Mexico City, home to a wide array of high-end boutiques, the most sought-after urban residences, the most important museums in the capital city and a vibrant, cosmopolitan atmosphere.

Its convenient location is ideal when planning visits to all the landmarks and attractions the city has to offer.

A few meters from The Alest is Parque Lincoln, a park that can be considered the heart of the neighborhood. Here, locals engage in recreational activities such as model boat sailing and racing, visiting a weekly outdoor market and even enjoying cultural events at the open-air Angela Peralta Theater.
restaurants

A broad range of dining options

As distinctive feature of the area surrounding The Alest, guests can enjoy the benefits stemming from our alliances with restaurants such as L'Osteria del Becco, Belfiore, Butcher & Sons, Cipriani, Cuerno Masaryk, Emilio, Fogo de Chao, Harry's, Garum, Ivoire, Le Pain Quotidien, Limantour, Porfirios, Pubbelly, Puerto Madero, Pujol, Quintonil, Rosa Negra, Ryoshi and Tori Tori, among others.

Living Polanco

Polanco is the main place to enjoy different types of experiences, and it pleases all kinds of travelers. The most wonderful things take place in the neighborhood or very close to it.

The most prestigious events, such as the Haute Horlogerie Salon (SIAR), art week Zona MACO, the Mercedes Benz Fashion Week, the Longines Global Champions Tour equestrian meet, Design Week and Formula 1 races are only a few examples of what The Alest guests will be able to attend.
